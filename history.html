Early Steps

Gostyń, Poland

The adventure begins in a small Polish city, where I spent the first 19 years of my life.

During this time, I completed basic education in classes with a mathematical profile with not bad results.

However, for most of the time, I focused more on developing my character and building my personality.

I spent hundreds of hours playing games, which helped me develop basic skills such asstrategic thinking, problem-solving, stress resistance, cooperation, goal-setting, and many others.

I also spent a significant amount of time reading books, mainly in the fantasy genre, which helped me develop a better imagination, broaden my perspectives and helped me establish my life values.

A significant part of this period was also consumed by comics. It is not possible to read hundreds of shounen manga without coding my brain in such a way that I would never be able to give up.

I also had the opportunity to learn to play several instruments, mainly drums. Through this experience, I learned patience and discipline, increased my concentration and multitasking skills.

By the end of this period, my character was ready and willing for adventures and further exploration of the world.

College

October 2016 - Febuary 2020

WSB, Poznań, Poland

My first big adventure was studying at the Higher School of Banking in Poznań. I chose the Information Technology and Graphic Design profile because they were always topics close to me and I wanted to learn more about them.

In a short time, I greatly expanded my knowledge of the basics of computer science, databases, the history of IT, operating systems, networks, and websites. I also learned the basics of my first programming language, which was C#.

One of the major advantages of the university was the opportunity to learn Graphic Design. I learned to use programs such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. I also attended lectures on the history of art and completed a professional drawing course.

This knowledge allowed me to start an adventure as a freelance graphic designer, which I will tell more about in my next experiences.

The university, in addition to classes related to the chosen profile, also provided me with many lectures on economics, marketing, and e-commerce. This knowledge greatly expanded my perspectives and defined many future adventures.

After seven semesters, I completed my studies at this university as a IT Engineer with a specialization in Graphics and Multimedia.

First Internship

March 2018 - June 2018

ERP System, Poznań, Poland

One of the conditions for completing my education at the Higher School of Banking was to gain professional experience through an internship at a chosen IT-related company.

I decided to choose something related to databases, because it was a topic that I was fascinated with at the time.

So, I began my internship at the Poznań-based company "ERP System", which, as the name suggests, dealt with the implementation and support of ERP systems for companies.

The company was small, but it was full of experienced backend developers from whom I had the opportunity to learn a lot.

I significantly expanded my knowledge in areas such as: databases, data management, SQL and SQL server, cloud data, implementation and support of ERP systems. I also got familiar with the whole Comarch toolkit which I had to use on a daily basis.

My standard day at the company involved finding data for customers by entering appropriate functions in SQL, learning and using Comarch programs.

My first internship was definitely a worthwhile experience, I gained many backend skills, but I lacked the opportunity to show imagination and creativity, so I decided that this profession was ultimately not for me.

Direct Salesperson

June 2018 - September 2018

TakTyk, Darłowo, Poland

During my studies, I had the most time for adventures between semesters. I decided not to waste my vacation and went on my first season by the Polish sea. I didn't really know what I was getting into at the time, but I wanted to step out of my comfort zone and try something new.

The job I got was to directly sell military dog tag products to tourists at a mobile stand on the boardwalk in Darłowo.

It was the first time I had to deal with sales, so it was not an easy task, but I quickly learned the basics and became a little more skilled in this field every day.

To stand out from my group of coworkers, I started learning about sales techniques, negotiations and the products I was selling.

Thanks to this tactic, in a short time, I left my colleagues behind with my sales results.

After a month of work, as a way to kill boredom, I began to propose to my superiors how I could improve the company with my graphic skills.

In agreement with the employer, I took care of the visual identification of the company and its implementation.

My sales job ended with the summer season, but at the end of it, I received an offer to return next year, this time as a Team Leader.

Team Sales Leader

June 2019 - September 2019

TakTyk, Darłowo, Poland

I took up the offer I received the previous year and returned to work during the summer season the following year, this time as a team sales leader.

During some days I still handled direct sales as I did the year before, but in addition to that, I had many new tasks.

I led a team of a variable number of members, but no more than 6 people, located in 5 cities along the Polish coast. I performed tasks from different fields such as: motivation, conducting workshops and crisis support.

In addition, I also dealt with logistics, the settlement system, and the company's finances.

The task turned out to be much more difficult than I had anticipated and I was not prepared for everything when I began this job. It required me to constantly expand my knowledge in the aforementioned categories, but nothing teaches as effectively as practice.

Once I had stabilized in my new position and my team was achieving satisfactory results, I continued my work on building the brand. I designed new sales points, created social media for the company, and engaged employees in content acquisition.

During this season, I learned a lot not only about team management but also about running a business. The possibility of starting my own business in the future seemed increasingly realistic.

Ultimately, the season under my management ended very successfully for the company, despite my lack of experience, I was able to meet the tasks set for me.

As with the previous year, at the end of the season, I received an offer to return next year, this time as a company manager.

Business Co-Owner

July 2021 - October 2021

Ale Bania!, Darłowo, Poland

The season on the Polish coast ended halfway when I quit working for the Vandam company. However, I did not want to lose the rest of the season, so I decided to open my own shop with a friend and a familiar investor.

In order to do this, I had to create a business model as quickly as possible, plan a budget, prepare the sales point from scratch, establish contact with many wholesalers, and start selling as soon as possible.

In addition, I had to prepare all the creative materials, such as the logo, banner design, and POS materials and order the appropriate prints.

With the help of friends and colleagues, we were able to achieve all of this in a matter of days.

The shop that we opened sold bubble-related toys. I came up with the idea of using a machine that releases soap bubbles with non-toxic smoke to enchant tourists walking through the boardwalk and encourage them to buy products.

The idea turned out to be a success, the promenade was filled with fascinating bubbles for children and sales were growing.

For some of the time, I personally took care of sales and service at the point, in addition to ordering and transporting products, managing the company's finances and logistics. Ultimately, at the end of the season, the profit from the sale of products exceeded the costs of maintaining the company, but only slightly.

I therefore did not achieve too much financial profit from this period, but I gained experience that I would not have been able to easily obtain.

After this adventure, I decided to take a break from coastal seasons and find more stable employment.
